# Custodial Account Guide (U.S. → International)

Welcome to the complete guide for immigrant parents who want to open, manage, and maintain a **U.S. custodial account (UGMA/UTMA)** for their child — even if they later relocate **internationally**.

This GitHub Pages site includes:

- Overview of custodial accounts  
- UGMA vs UTMA  
- Custodial account vs 529 plan  
- International strategy  
- Fidelity & Schwab setup guides  
- Tax considerations (U.S. → India)  
- Investment portfolio examples  
- Maintenance checklist  
- Lifecycle flow diagram
