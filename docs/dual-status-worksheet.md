## Critical Allocation Rules
1. **Dividends:** Income is recognized on the **Payable Date**, not the Ex-Dividend date. If the dividend was paid the day after you left, it is Non-Resident income.
2. **Capital Gains:** Gains are recognized on the **Trade Date** (date of sale).
3. **Interest:** Usually allocated based on the number of days you were a resident vs. non-resident, or based on the date the interest was credited to the account.

## Reminders
- Dual-status taxpayers cannot use the **Standard Deduction**.
- Ensure Form **W-8BEN** was on file with the broker for the Non-Resident period to ensure correct withholding.
- Keep this worksheet with your 1099-B and 1099-DIV forms for at least 7 years.
