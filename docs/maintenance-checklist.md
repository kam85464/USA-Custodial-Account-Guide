# Maintenance Checklist

## Before Leaving the U.S.
- Open custodial account  
- Maintain U.S. bank account  
- Set U.S. mailing address  
- **Designate Successor Custodian** (Required for continuity)
- Store documents  
- Enable 2FA  

## After Moving Abroad
- Keep U.S. address active  
- Keep U.S. bank active  
- Continue investing  
- Withdraw only for child’s benefit  
- Review Successor Custodian details (Annually)
- Track taxes  

## At Majority Age
- Child verifies identity online  
- Ownership transfers  
- Child decides: invest or withdraw
