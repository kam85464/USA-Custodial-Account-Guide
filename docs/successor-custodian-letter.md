# Sample Designation of Successor Custodian

*This template is for use when a brokerage does not provide a standard online form. It should be signed in the presence of a Notary Public.*

---

**To:** [Brokerage Name, e.g., Charles Schwab / Fidelity]  
**Account Number:** [Your Account Number]  
**Date:** [Current Date]

**Subject: Designation of Successor Custodian for UGMA/UTMA Account**

I, **[Your Full Legal Name]**, currently serving as the Custodian for **[Minor's Full Legal Name]** under the **[State, e.g., California]** Uniform Transfers to Minors Act (UTMA), hereby designate a Successor Custodian to act in the event of my resignation, death, or incapacity.

**Successor Custodian Details:**
- **Name:** [Successor's Full Legal Name]
- **Relationship to Minor:** [e.g., Mother / Uncle]
- **Address:** [Successor's Physical Address]
- **Phone Number:** [Successor's Phone Number]

This designation is made pursuant to the laws of the State of **[State Name]**. I request that the brokerage update its records to reflect this designation.

Sincerely,

__________________________  
**[Your Signature]**  
[Your Printed Name]
