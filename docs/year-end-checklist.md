# Year-End Tax & Maintenance Checklist

Run through this checklist every **December** to optimize the custodial account for taxes and security.

## 1. Tax Optimization
- [ ] **Check Kiddie Tax Thresholds:** Review total unearned income (dividends + realized gains). Is it approaching the **$2,600** (2024) limit?
- [ ] **Tax Loss Harvesting:** If gains are high, sell underperforming assets at a loss to offset those gains.
- [ ] **Gift Tax Exclusion:** Ensure total contributions for the year don't exceed **$18,000** per donor (2024 limit) to avoid filing Form 709.

## 2. Maintenance & Compliance
- [ ] **Verify Address:** Confirm the U.S. mailing address is still active and valid.
- [ ] **Verify W-8BEN Expiration:** Form W-8BEN generally expires on December 31st of the third succeeding calendar year after it was signed. Check your records to see if a refresh is needed for the coming year.
- [ ] **Check 2FA:** Ensure your U.S. phone number (Google Voice/Tello) is active and you can receive login codes.

## 3. Financial Planning
- [ ] **Rebalance Portfolio:** If stocks have grown significantly, consider selling some to buy bonds/cash to maintain your target allocation (Medium/Short horizons).
- [ ] **Review Successor:** Ensure your designated Successor Custodian is still the correct person.

## 4. Documentation
- [ ] **Download Statements:** Save the year's monthly statements to your secure, encrypted vault.
- [ ] **Preview 1099s:** Most brokers provide "Tax Year-to-Date" views; use this to estimate next year's tax bill.
