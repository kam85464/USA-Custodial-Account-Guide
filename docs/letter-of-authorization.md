# Letter of Authorization (Mailing Address)

*This document is a private agreement between the account owner and the friend providing the U.S. mailing address. It helps clarify that the friend is not the owner of the assets and is simply facilitating mail forwarding.*

---

**To:** [Friend's Full Name]  
**From:** [Custodian's Full Name]  
**Date:** [Date]  

**Subject: Authorization to Receive and Forward Financial Correspondence**

I, **[Custodian's Name]**, as the legal custodian for the account of **[Child's Name]**, hereby authorize **[Friend's Name]** to receive financial mail and correspondence on our behalf at the following address:

**[U.S. Physical Address]**

**Terms of Agreement:**
1. **Mail Handling:** [Friend's Name] is authorized to receive mail from [Brokerage Name, e.g., Fidelity/Schwab]. 
2. **Notification:** [Friend's Name] agrees to notify the custodian of any urgent or official-looking mail within [Number, e.g., 7] days of receipt.
3. **No Ownership:** This letter confirms that [Friend's Name] has no legal or beneficial interest in the assets held within the custodial account. 
4. **Duration:** This authorization remains in effect until [Date] or until revoked in writing by the Custodian.

**Custodian Signature:**  
__________________________  

**Friend Acknowledgment:**  
__________________________
