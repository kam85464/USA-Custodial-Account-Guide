# Successor Custodian: Letter of Intent

*This document is intended to accompany your formal Successor Custodian designation. While the formal form gives legal authority, this letter provides the "why" and "how" for the person taking over.*

---

**To:** [Successor Custodian Name]  
**Re:** Management of Custodial Account for [Minor's Name]  

Dear [Name],

I have designated you as the Successor Custodian for the [Fidelity/Schwab] UGMA/UTMA account held for [Minor's Name]. Should I become unable to manage this account, please follow these guidelines:

1. **Purpose of Funds:** This account was established to provide for [Minor's Name]'s [Education/Future/First Home]. Please prioritize long-term growth over immediate liquidity unless there is an emergency.
2. **International Considerations:** As we are living outside the U.S., please be aware that the brokerage may require you to submit a Form W-8BEN and prove your identity via a U.S. Consulate.
3. **Investment Philosophy:** The current portfolio consists of [e.g., Low-cost broad market ETFs]. I prefer that these remain invested to take advantage of compounding, rather than frequent trading.
4. **Tax Compliance:** Please ensure that annual tax reporting is completed in both the U.S. (Kiddie Tax) and our current country of residence.
5. **Transfer at Majority:** When [Minor's Name] reaches age [18/21/25], please assist them in taking full legal ownership of the assets.

Thank you for your willingness to support [Minor's Name]'s financial future.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
