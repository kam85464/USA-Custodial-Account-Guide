# Custodial Account vs 529 Plan (Immigrant-Focused)

## Comparison

| Feature | Custodial Account | 529 Plan |
|--------|-------------------|----------|
| Usage | Any child-benefit expense | Education-only |
| Flexibility | Very high | Low |
| Tax benefits | None special | Strong U.S. tax benefits |
| Control | Child gets control at 18–25 | Parent controls forever |
| Best for | Families who may relocate internationally | U.S. education certainty |

## Conclusion
If you may move abroad, a custodial account is superior due to flexibility and global usability.
