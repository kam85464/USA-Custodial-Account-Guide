# Investment Portfolio (Educational Example)

## Long Horizon (10+ years)
- 80–90% stock ETFs  
- 10–20% bond ETFs  

## Medium Horizon (5–10 years)
- 60–70% stock ETFs  
- 30–40% bond ETFs  

## Short Horizon (<5 years)
- 20–40% stock ETFs  
- 60–80% bonds/cash
