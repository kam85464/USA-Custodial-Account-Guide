# Lifecycle Flowchart

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A([Start: Decide to Invest for Child]) --> B[Choose UGMA/UTMA]
    B --> C[Select Provider: Fidelity or Schwab]
    C --> D[Gather Documents<br/>IDs, SSNs, Address]
    D --> E[Open Account Online]
    E --> F[Fund via U.S. Bank]
    F --> G[Invest in ETFs / Index Funds]
    G --> H[Manage & Rebalance Over Years]

    H --> I{Move Abroad?}
    I -->|Yes| J[Maintain U.S. Address & Bank]
    J --> K[Continue Investing from Abroad]
    K --> L[Withdraw Only for Child's Benefit]

    L --> M[Child Reaches Majority Age 18–25]
    M --> N[Transfer Ownership Online from Abroad]
    N --> O([Child Chooses: Keep Investing or Withdraw])
```
